Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy when using our age difference calculator

Introduction

At AgeGapChecker.online, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our age gap calculator and related services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our age gap calculator is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be very clear about what information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or birth dates
  • We do not store any calculation results or inputs
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that personally identify users
  • We do not share any user data with third parties

How Our Calculator Works

Our age gap calculator operates entirely in your web browser. All calculations are performed locally on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers. This means:

  • Your birth dates and calculation results never leave your device
  • We have no way to access or view the information you enter
  • Your privacy is completely protected while using our tool

Information We May Collect

While we don't collect personal data through our calculator, we may collect limited anonymous information for website analytics:

  • Approximate geographic location (country level) based on IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website (if applicable)

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Web hosting providers to make our website accessible
  • Analytics services to understand how our website is used
  • Advertising networks to display relevant ads (if applicable)

These services may collect anonymous data as described above, but they do not have access to any personal information you enter into our age gap calculator.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited anonymous information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the bottom of this policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@agegapchecker.online
  • Through our contact form on the website
